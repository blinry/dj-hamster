== A Bloody Small World ==

This is a racing game that takes place inside a human blood stream. You are teaching a class of red blood cells how to take oxygen from the lungs to the organs.

Made together with Moritz, Pecca, and Winnie for Ludum Dare 38. Find the game at morr.cc/bloody/.

Winnie produced the soundtrack for this game. It consists of the songs "Blood Cell Intro", "Blood Cell Party", and "Blood Cell Blues" (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0).
